Why Aakruthi hits different?

  • Flagship technical events: Prasthuthi, Prakalpana, Pradarshana, Medha, Trishna, Yantra.
  • Chanakya & Laghuchitra for general quizzers and storytellers.
  • Aahladakara & Sangrahalaya to unwind, perform, and explore classic electronics.

Paper Presentation Team / Solo

Prasthuthi

Research presentations in front of academia & R&D experts.

Present structured technical papers highlighting your problem statement, methodology, and results to an expert jury. Great for research‑oriented students who love surveys, experimentation, and writing publishable work.

Tracks: VLSI · Communication · Embedded · AI‑IoT Register
Project Expo Prototypes

Prakalpana

Stage for projects waiting to go global.

Showcase working hardware, software, or hybrid projects that solve real‑world problems using creative engineering. Explain your design decisions, implementation journey, and how your solution scales beyond campus.

Categories: Hardware · Software · Hybrid Register
Poster Presentation Visual

Pradarshana

Poster contest to measure the spread of ideas.

Translate complex concepts into crisp, visually appealing posters with diagrams, flows, and key results. Ideal for early‑stage work and concept explorations that benefit from strong visual storytelling.

Judging: Idea · Design · Clarity Register
Coding Challenge Timed

Medha

Prove your excellence in coding.

Tackle problem‑solving rounds that test algorithms, debugging, and implementation efficiency. Expect problems inspired by automation, data pipelines, and embedded workflows.

Languages: C · C++ · Java · Python Register
Technical Quiz Multi‑round

Trishna

Quizotic technocrat revelation.

Dive into an enigmatic quiz featuring core ECE, tech trends, and fun engineering trivia. Written, visual, and buzzer rounds reward both depth of knowledge and fast thinking.

Focus: ECE · Tech · Trivia Register
Robotics Arena

Yantra

Invigorating robotic battle event.

Bring your bots to a high‑voltage arena filled with tracks, obstacles, and battle formats. Test speed, control, robustness, and creativity against rival teams’ machines.

Experience Zone Gallery

Sangrahalaya

Stage for traditional electronics equipment.

Explore curated collections of classic instruments and devices that shaped the evolution of electronics. Connect past technologies with today’s high‑speed systems through live demos and stories.

Theme: History of ECE Participate

General Quiz Open
