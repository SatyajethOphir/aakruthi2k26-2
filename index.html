Founded by a Visionary Professor.
Built for Generations of Engineers.

The Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering was established in 1959 at the University College of Engineering, Osmania University ‚Äî with just two faculty members and an unshakeable vision. It bears the indelible stamp of its founder and first Head of Department, Late Prof. K. Krishnan Nair, who exhorted the ECE Department to become a byname for Excellence, Creativity and Enterprise. In his memory, the Commemoration Committee organises an annual lecture by a distinguished industry professional. Over six decades, the department has grown into a thriving hub of 16 teaching faculty, 500+ students, and specialisations spanning Digital Systems, Signal Processing, VLSI, Microwaves, Embedded Systems, and Communications Engineering.
